Soul Searching is an application for listening to your music files.

With the latter, you can:

- manage your music, folders, albums and artists
- access the lyrics (both normal and synced) of the played music
- manage several artists linked to a music
- create playlists
- manage your played list
- see statistics on your listenings
- use a quick access system

The application also offers several customization elements (color theme, player view...).
